This mini-site was created in collaboration with Minh Cao as part of a research presentation on the typeface Futura. We chose to use a website as our medium of presentation in order to create an engaging experiences for the audience that focused on the key aspects of the typeface which make it iconic. The website implies interactivity and turns the presentation into an activity. The chosen aspects of Futura that we focused on were its geometries, usage in pop culture, and the variety of fonts within the superfamily.
